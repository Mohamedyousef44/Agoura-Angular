Our Story

Agoura is a marketplace website that makes it easy for people to safely sell and buy their homes. Launched in 2023, the Agoura website has been visited by millions of people in Egypt and other countries.

At Agoura, our mission is to create value in a global marketplace where anyone can buy and sell. We believe that by creating a smarter, more sustainable way to shop, we can help people live better lives.

We are committed to reducing the waste of time by giving the ability to buy and sell from everywhere, and to make this more accessible by connecting people around the world. Our team of over 500 employees is dedicated to creating a better, more connected future.

Our Values

Empower Everyone

We believe that everyone should have the opportunity to participate in the global economy, regardless of where they live or what they have to sell.

Be Bold

We believe that innovation is key to creating a better world, and we are not afraid to take risks and try new things.

Do the Right Thing

We believe that honesty, integrity, and transparency are essential to building trust with our users and creating a sustainable business.
